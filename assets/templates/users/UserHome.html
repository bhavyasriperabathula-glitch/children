Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder

Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by symptoms of inattention, hyperactivity, impulsivity, and emotional dysregulation.

According to studies, the prevalence of symptomatic adult ADHD was about 6.76% globally.

The exact cause of ADHD is unknown, but genetics and brain structure may play an important role.
